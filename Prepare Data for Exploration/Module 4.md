## Key Takeaways (Bring data to order)

- **Benefits of organizing data**:
	- Make it easier to find and use
	- Helps you avoid making mistakes during your analysis
	- Helps to protect your data

- **Best practices when organizing data**: [[File organization guidelines]]
	- **Naming Conventions**: Use logical and descriptive names for files to enhance searchability and usability.
	
	- **Foldering**: Organize files into folders to keep related project files together, making them easier to access.
	
	- **Archiving older files**: Move older projects to a separate location to reduce clutter and improve accessibility.
	
	- **Team Alignment**: Ensure naming and storage practices are consistent within a team to avoid confusion and develop metadata practices.
