## Key Takeaways (Take action with data)

- **Case Study on Data Analytics**:
	- **Understanding the Business Problem**
		- The case focuses on Anywhere Gaming Repair, a business seeking to expand its customer base through effective advertising.
		- The owner was uncertain about which advertising methods would best reach their target audience of video game system owners.
	
	- **Data Analysis Process**
		- Data analyst Maria began by defining the problem, collaborating with stakeholders to identify the real issue: understanding the target audience's preferred advertising methods.
		- In the preparation phase, Maria collected data on the target audience and various advertising methods to determine the most effective approach.
	
	- **Recommendations and Results**
		- After analyzing the data, Maria identified that individuals aged 18-34 were the primary audience and that podcasts were a cost-effective advertising medium.
		- The business implemented her recommendation, resulting in a significant increase in customers after running a podcast ad for a month.

## Key Takeaways (Solve problems with data)

- **Types of Problems in Data Analysis** [[Six common problem types]]:
	1. **Making Predictions**: Involves using data to forecast future events, such as a hospital predicting health issues for patients based on their daily health vitals.
	
	2. **Categorizing Things**: Grouping data based on common features. Assigning data into groups based on shared characteristics, like a manufacturer grouping employees by their performance in different tasks.
	
	(Identifying and Analyzing Patterns)
	
	3. **Spotting Something Unusual**: Identifying anomalies in data, such as a sudden increase in student registrations due to new housing developments.
	
	4. **Identifying Themes**: Grouping data into broader concepts, like categorizing employee performance into low and high productivity.
	
	(Connecting Insights)
	
	5. **Discovering Connections**: Finding similarities in challenges faced by different entities, which can lead to collaborative solutions, such as a scooter company and its wheel supplier facing similar issues
	
	6. **Finding Patterns**: Analyzing historical data to identify trends, like e-commerce companies understanding customer buying habits during specific times of the year.

## Key Takeaways (Craft effective questions)

- Effective Questioning Techniques [[SMART Questions]]
	- Questions should be **specific, measurable, action-oriented, relevant, and time-bound (SMART)** to guide analysis effectively.
	- Closed-ended and leading questions can limit insights; instead, open-ended questions encourage deeper understanding.

- Fairness in Questioning
	- Fairness is Ensuring that your questions don't create or reinforce bias.
	- Fair questions avoid bias and assumptions, ensuring clarity and reliability in responses.
	- Crafting questions that everyone can understand is crucial for gathering valuable feedback.
